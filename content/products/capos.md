# Overview

CapOS is Neon AI Cloud’s **capability operating system**—a local-first application that enforces a closed operating loop for serious technical work:

**Plan → Execute → Prove → Review → Publish → Compound**

It is built for practitioners and teams who need more than task lists: every day of work should produce **artefacts**, **evidence**, and a durable trail of capability growth.

## What it does

CapOS binds strategy to daily execution through an instrumented operating portal:

- **Planning** ranks curriculum and project options against capability goals
- **Daily check-in** records study, build work, artefacts, proof, blockers, and tomorrow’s commitment
- **Evidence ledger** links proof to projects, milestones, skills, and check-ins
- **Artifacts and publications** convert accepted work into portfolio and authority outputs
- **Reviews** generate weekly, monthly, and quarterly assessments from the evidence trail
- **Coach and health** surfaces support critique and data integrity without replacing human ownership

## Operating rule

A day is not complete because time was spent. A day is complete when progress is **backed by evidence**—or an explicit, recorded exception.

**Desired capability → Active project → Today’s proof target → Evidence → Review → Next focus**

## Who it is for

- Engineers and architects building durable AI, platform, and infrastructure capability
- Solo operators and small teams who need an evidence-oriented operating rhythm
- Organisations that want capability development to compound into portfolio and commercial readiness

## Engagement shape

CapOS can be deployed as a local-first operating environment for individuals or teams, with Neon AI Cloud configuring curriculum, operating standards, and review cadence as part of a capability engagement.

## Technical posture

Local-first web application, structured domain model, deterministic service modules for check-ins, evidence, dashboard aggregation, and reviews. Designed for operational clarity—not as a generic productivity SaaS skin.
