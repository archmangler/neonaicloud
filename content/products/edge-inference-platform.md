# Overview

The Edge Inference Platform packages model serving, device software, and operational baselines for environments where power, latency, and reliability constraints are first-class.

## What you get

- Device and edge runtime architecture
- Integration patterns for sensors, actuators, and host systems
- Validation evidence under representative conditions
- Handover artefacts for long-term ownership

## Engagement

Work proceeds from workload and constraint discovery through design, implementation, and validation. The outcome is an operable platform—not a one-off prototype.
