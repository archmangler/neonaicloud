We implement a complete digital twin of a company employee or role based on a database provided by you.
One use of this type of a.i agent is to act as a customer service bot integrated with your company website.
However, we find a wide range of use-cases and applications of a.i agents trained on employee role descriptions to carry out customer-facing tasks based on a store of company data (e.g staff training manuals, job descriptions, email mail boxes).

Neon AI will help you design, test, validate and deploy your first working MVP of such digital twins, as well as build a production environment to host it and the deployment tooling to update, upgrade and run your agent in production!
